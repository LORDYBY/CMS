Database migration with alembic (empty DB)
==========================================


1 - alembic current         #Check DB state#
2 - alembic stamp head       #Align Alembic metadata with DB#
3 - alembic revision --autogenerate -m "initial schema"  #Generate initial migration#
4 - alembic upgrade head        #Apply migration to DB#
=================================================

NB: Important rule (do NOT misuse)
==================================
